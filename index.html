FAQs for book publishers

How can I submit my manuscript for consideration?

To submit your manuscript for consideration, please visit our submissions page on our website. Follow the guidelines provided to ensure your submission is complete and meets our requirements.

What genres do you publish?

We publish a wide range of genres, including fiction (mystery, romance, science fiction, fantasy, literary, etc.), non-fiction (biography, memoir, self-help, etc.), and more. Check our catalog for a better idea of the genres we specialize in.

Do you accept unsolicited manuscripts?

Yes, we welcome unsolicited manuscripts from both established and aspiring authors. However, we recommend reviewing our submission guidelines to increase your chances of acceptance.

What is the typical timeline for reviewing a manuscript submission?

The review timeline can vary, but we aim to respond to submissions within [X weeks/months]. Please be patient, as our team carefully evaluates each manuscript to provide thoughtful feedback.

What is the publishing process like if my manuscript is accepted?

If your manuscript is accepted, it will undergo a thorough editing process, including content and copy editing. We will also work on cover design, formatting, and publication. The timeline for each stage may vary depending on the book's complexity

How much control will I have over the editing and design of my book?

We believe in collaboration and value your input. Our team will work closely with you during the editing and design phases to ensure your vision is respected while maintaining the highest quality standards.
